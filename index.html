RepairoFixer is an apartment facilities provider firm. They won a contract to provide facility maintenance service across 1000+ government sponsored housing complexes.

The company has a large list of sub-contractors (150+). And it takes the resource allocation manager a lot of time to find the best to do a specific job. Hence, they want to automate this process and let the field service staff self-organize and find work proactively using a chatbot. Speed to finding and accepting a suitable work could be super crucial.
